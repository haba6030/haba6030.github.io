About Me

Hi! I'm an undergraduate student at Seoul National University, majoring in Linguistics, Psychology, and Computer Science.

I aim to develop computational frameworks that extract latent signatures from human brain and behavior, and translate them into practical systems that can:

  • Support early and reliable diagnosis of mental and cognitive states,
  • Personalize interventions based on individual brain–behavior profiles,
  • Improve artificial intelligence by incorporating principles of human learning and adaptation,
  • Uncover shared and distinct features across brains, machines, languages, and the systems that structure human life.
